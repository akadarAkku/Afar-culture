Muggaaqinna:

Xaaleena yoobukeh yan baxah yayyeqen/fooca fan kaah elle seecan migaaqa.
Qafar ayyuntih addal xaylo elle muggaaqisaanam kataysis kee ramad kinnuk xaleyna(Ina kee abba) ina soonibah tan uddur sittageyan numul/barral muggaaqisan.
Too uddur ullah ullaalooh wadaraay,laa,gaala,illi, w.w tanim yarciiden,too uddur awki/awka muggaqti/to awqenta kellih mekla.Ken catak ayyuntiinoh fantaawak isik maqarroysaah,muggaaqinnaak ugutak kedooy buxa marih kacanu,sitta cataanam kee sitta qikak xiinamih caalat ken fanat ginniima.

Farankaqtu፡

Farankaqtu Kak yaanam qafar addal baxah kataysa ginoonuh isinni baxa dareefal mamaxxaga leh yan numuk filla/lakbal daffeysak/sunkul kaak kursak yi baxi ku kataysa axcuk yaaban,awka kaa elle heen num too ayrok qimbisak isi uddurul yaaxige.
Too num awkah acwa yaceeh,awka kee kaa elle heen num yi kataysa axcuk ittah seecan.
Tohuuk ugutak namma kedo kee buxâ marih fanat kaxxa qalla ginah yan gexsit ginniima.
