Muggaaqinna፡-

Xaaline yoobukeh yan baxa yayyeqen/fooca fan kaah elle seecan migaaqa.
Qafar ayyuntih addal xaylo elle muggaaqisaanam kataysis kee ramad kinnuk xaleyna(Ina kee abba)
ina soonibah tan uddur sittageyan numul/barral muggaaqisan.Too uddur ullah ullaalooh wadaraay,laa,gaala,illi, w.w
tanim yarciiden,too uddur awki/awka muggaqti/to awqenta kellih mekla.
Ken catak ayyuntiinoh fantaawak isik maqarroysaah,muggaaqinnaak ugutak kedooy buxa marih kacanu,sitta cataanam kee
sitta qikak xiinamih caalat ken fanat ginniima.

Farankaqtu:-

Farankaqtu Kak yaanam qafar addal baxah kataysa Kak yaanam qafar addal baxah kataysa ፡
ginoonuh isinni baxa dareefal mamaxxaga leh yan numuk filla/lakbal daffeysak/sunkul kaak kursak
yi baxi ku kataysa axcuk yaaban,awka kaa elle heen num too ayrok qimbisak isi uddurul yaaxige.
Too num awkah acwa yaceeh,awka kee kaa elle heen num yi kataysa axcuk ittah seecan.
Tohuuk ugutak namma kedo kee buxâ marih fanat kaxxa qalla ginah yan gexsit ginniima.

Onqoru ፡-

Onqoru ፡- Kak yaanam alaaqa taabukeh tan waqdi keenih abaanam kinnuk onqor baxaabaxsale gurral abaanah,baxsale maqna akah yacen.
Onqor akah abaanam agiru yakku kee ummatta xaquk kicnon num yakkuh aban.
Onqor abtam ummatta xaqul mamaxxaga leh yan num kinnuk akah aban awki(agiru, gaddali, darsaytu/qaalim, kedoh abba, makaabantu)
xaleyna kay innah kunnaba le num yoh akkele yaanamih kibal haak abscissa.
Awki mamaxxaga le num yekkek xaleyna hebli yoh onqorise baxi agiru,qaalim, kedoh abba, makaabantu yoh yekke axcuk ken fanat kacanu
kee qalli ginniimaah,keenik fulak ken kedo kee buxâ marih fanat ginniima ellacabo sinnih qalli ciggilta horal tabaataba.

Aliginnu:

Aliginna kak yaanam qafar ayyuntih addal digbah/rihimi/ yan qaarus hinnay qaruusah aliginna kinnuk aligge kee qaruus itta ceela sara saritaanam kee nabam bilqitak farcitan.Aliginni qafar ayyuntih addal namma exxal kurruuma.Tohuuy aligge kee aliggeyta axcuk muggaaqisan.Aligge kak yaanam lab num kinnuk aliggeyta kaadu say num aban migaaqa. Qaarus lakqok yayyaaqemil kaa catak awqenta kee farci ittallih aban.Tahaak ugutak qafar ayyuntih addal maqarreeh naba geemat leh yan ayyunttiinoh fantaaxaw kinnuk aligge kee qarus kedooda kee buxâ marih fanat maqarre qalli ginniima.
